— Who We Are

Dots Group of Companies is a Pan African Resources Investment Company incorporated in Ghana. The company is headquartered in Accra, Ghana, and has entered into strategic partnerships with several entities in furtherance of its objectives. Dots Group of Companies has four major service offerings across these industries: Agribusiness – agro-processing, Commodity trading, Logistics and shipping, Advertising and Marketing.

Dots group of Companies team is distinguished by a very strong technical background, as well as effective operational and financial expertise. These qualities are complemented by the combined experience of the team and our partners, who continue to focus on delivering professional and quality experience for our clients.

— Our Products

Our agribusiness offering ensures sustainable and efficient agricultural production in key commodities such as maize and cocoa farming as well as livestock breeding to contribute to food security in Africa. Dots Group of Companies agribusiness ensures responsible farming through the use of environmentally friendly products for crop production and breeding. Our objective is to take a holistic approach and create integrated processes to produce from the ground to the shop shelves. We aim to cooperate with industry partners, governments, NGOs, and communities to support the achievement of the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
